About Us !

COVID has added safety restrictions for stores. We need to make sure that the store isn’t too crowded and that the lines don’t have too many people on it. Line Up is an Innovative ticket system Allow people to sign up online, in person, and over the phone. The tickets numbers will be given starting from 1. People coming in person or are calling over the phone will have their spot on-the-line tracked by the online system. Ticket numbers are first come first serve. The smaller your ticket number, the closer you are to the front of the line.
